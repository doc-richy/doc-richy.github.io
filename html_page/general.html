General informations for the Yo game.

The main goal is to help Yo to hide bunches of bananas with boxes. He must push box over bananas to hide them in the forest. When box reaches bunches of bananas, they disappear into the wood.

There are 3 differents scenes, 12x12 , 14x14 and 16x16. They mean the territory width and height where Yo can move, but only 12x12 grid is visible for the gamer. Yo can move on sand and bananas tiles, the forest is too dense and dangerous. Each scene have 9 levels. Choose your scene and level and hits the play button. In scene 12x12, Yo can only pushes one box at a time. In scenes 14x14 and 16x16, Yo becomes stronger and can push two boxes at once.

To move Yo on keyboard, arrow keys, "wasd" and "ijkl" keys can be used. For touch screen, use arrows on the screen.

To get access to the next level, you must complete the playing one. The hint button is active only if the player have done 5 attempts. One try consist of hiding at least half of bunches of bananas and walking half of suggested steps in the target column.

Yo must do that within a minimum of time and a minimum of steps.

Scene 16x16 level 2 and up are available only for signed user.
