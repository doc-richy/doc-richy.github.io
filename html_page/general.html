General informations for the Yo game.

The main goal is to help Yo to hide his bananas grapes with boxes. He must push box over bananas grape to hide them in the forest. When box reach bananas grape, it disappear.

There are 3 differents scenes, 12x12 , 14x14 and 16x16. They mean the territory width and height where Yo can move, but only 12x12 grid is visible for the gamer. Yo can move only on the sand tiles, the forest is too dense and dangerous. Each scene have 9 levels. Choose your scene and level and hits the play button.

To get access to the next level, you must complete the playing level.

Yo must do that within a minimum of time and a minimum of foot steps.

Scene 16x16 and level 2 and up are available only for signed user.

Buttons meaning.

This is the main menu. It includes fullscreen, sound and music buttons. The player must activate them each time he signs in.

This button toggle beetween fullscreen mode and not fullscreen. The game is better view in fullscreen mode and optimized for full HD, 1920x1024.

To have sounds in the game, you must click it.

To have music in the game, this button must be activated.

This button stops the game and shows all others non-visibles ones. You can take a break and think strategic moves to go on.

Go back to the main screen.

You can reload the game when you are stuck and the puzzle can not be resolved anymore.

You can store or reload anytime during the game. Only one saved game is allowed for each level.

You can go to the next level only if the playing one is completed.

This button shows informations of the live game, the "NOW" column, and your best score until than. The best score is updated only for completed level. The last column is a hint target for both, the time and the number of steps. Theses hints are not optimized, only for reference. Try to beat them.
